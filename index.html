Pangolins

Pangolins are the most trafficked animal in the world.

It's tragic because these animals are amazing.

Pangolin species

There are eight species of pangolins.

  • Ground Pangolin
  • Chinese Pangolin
  • Black-bellied pangolin
  • White-bellied pangolin
  • Giant pangolin
  • Sunda pangolin
  • Phillipine pangolin
  • Indian pangolin

Trafficking

This is what people do with pangolins/their parts.

  1. Eat them.
  2. Harvest their scales.
  3. Make pangolin wine.
Don't you think that Lorek, from HBO's His Dark Materials looks like a pangolin in his armor?

How to help

Because pangolins live in Asia and Africa, it's easy to feel that there's nothing you can do to help. However, knowledge is power, so feel free to study up on pangolins and let others know about their plight.

A big thanks to SavePangolins.org for such helpful information.
